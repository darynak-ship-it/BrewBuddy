Privacy Policy for BrewBuddy

Effective date: September 15, 2025

BrewBuddy values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

No Personal Data Collected

BrewBuddy does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data such as timers, ratings, and notes are stored locally on your device.

Notifications

BrewBuddy uses local notifications to remind you about fermentation timers. These notifications are generated and managed entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

BrewBuddy does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

BrewBuddy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
